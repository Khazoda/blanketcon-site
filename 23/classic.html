BlanketCon 23: cyber/NOVA is an in-game Minecraft modding convention hosted by ModFest, QuiltMC, and CHS and open to all mod enthusiasts and developers! Come check out a custom modpack and convention world full of mods of all kinds - old favorites, new up-and-comers, hidden gems, and more! Now in its second year and powered by Quilt on Minecraft 1.20, we're here to show off the future of Minecraft modding and celebrate the community!

Featuring:

  • A pack full of mods of all levels of notability!
  • A custom Neo Lisbon showcase world built by the team and participants!
  • Panels, keynotes, and more from modders and community members!
  • Booths, demos, games, and more showing off everything modded Minecraft has to offer!
  • The crushing ennui of late-stage capitalism! I don't think that was on the guest list...

Booth and event applications are now open! Check in the Discord or visit this link to apply: blanketcon.modfest.net/signup
Feel free to submit applications for as many booths or events as you'd like! Mods and developers for any mod loader are welcome to create booths and hold events! However, due to technical restrictions, we can only add Quilt and Fabric mods to the pack itself.

Making resource packs, datapacks, community resources? Even if it's only vaguely Minecraft-related, we encourage you to make a submission!
